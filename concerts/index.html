1. Scrape

Venue adapters pull public calendars — the Mississippi Studios / Revolution Hall feeds and the Ticketmaster Discovery API for the rest of Portland and Seattle — into one event shape.

2. Merge

Shows are de-duplicated by artist, date, and venue, hand-picked entries are folded in, and everything past today is dropped.

3. Publish

The result is written to shows.ics, sorted by date, and served here and to any calendar app you subscribe.
