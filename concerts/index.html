Adapters normalize venue, promoter, and ticket-page calendars into one event shape: artist, support, venue, city, date, URL, onsale state, and source confidence.

2. Listen

3. Alert

The scorer boosts artists you play often, artists you collect, related openers, rare visits, Portland/Seattle proximity, and shows nearing sellout.
