A Parliament of Neighbors

A live participatory piece on worldbuilding, speculative governance, and what we owe our neighbors.

A live event paired with a website that ran a sticky-note feature, opening the piece to anyone who wanted to put themselves into the idea. The site is offline now. The documentation video remains.

Bringing worldbuilding and speculative futures into community spaces. What if governance and community were a blank canvas? How would we choose to exist, live, and play together?

Most reimagination of cities focuses on physical infrastructure. This interactive experience asked us to think about the people who make a place.

The piece zoomed in to what it means to govern, and what our responsibilities are to our neighbors.
