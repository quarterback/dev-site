Forward Deployed Design

Forward-deployed design is the operating model I ran for eight years at 18F — four of them as director of a 40-person design division: designers embedded where problems actually happen, on short, problem-scoped, cross-functional engagements that ship working product, not handoffs.

I’m going deeper on it here — more writing, more argument, and the wider conversation it belongs to. Working this way, or think it’s wrong? Tell me.
